# Security Policy

Caustic is an independent security research lab. Report vulnerabilities in Caustic-owned repositories and infrastructure through the channel below.

## Reporting

Email security@caustic.sh. Encrypt sensitive reports with the PGP public key at https://github.com/causticsec/.github/blob/main/pgp/security.asc (fingerprint `AD37 C0AE 1EF0 8406 B3DB 3DAA B8CF B4A6 229A 2A95`).

Include:

- Affected component and version.
- Impact.
- Reproduction steps or proof of concept.

## Scope

Caustic-owned repositories and infrastructure. Do not test against systems Caustic does not own or control without explicit authorization.

## Safe harbor

Caustic will not pursue legal action against good-faith research conducted in accordance with the disclosure policy and reported through the official channel.

## Disclosure

For how reports are triaged, coordinated, and published, see the [Coordinated Disclosure Policy](https://github.com/causticsec/.github/blob/main/VULNERABILITY_DISCLOSURE_POLICY.md).
